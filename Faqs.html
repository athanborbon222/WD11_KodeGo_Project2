Merchant

    How does the service work?
  • Each shop has its own page where they can promote their products or services. We have a dedicated local customer service team that are fluent in both English and Filipino to help make transactions easy.
    How do I join?
  • Interested merchants can send their inquiries to ask@chanjo.com. To maintain the highest standards, you may be asked to comply with CH•AN•JO requisites such as BIR Registration, deliverable items, consistent supply, approved packaging, among others.
    What is the merchant process?
  • From setting up your shop to providing you with the right data, PICK.A.CREW will be there to support you. We’ll get you on board in six easy steps, here’s how:
      Step 1: SIGN UP
    • Sign up and submit all requirements
      Step 2: SET UP SHOP
    • Or PICK.A.CREW will set up your shop.
      Step 3: GET TRAINED
    • We will train you and your team
      Step 4: GO LIVE
    • Your shop will go live.
      Step 5: GET INSIGHTS
    • We will analyze data and insights.
      Step 6: CREATE MARKETING PLAN
    • We will create a marketing plan for you.

For Riders

FAQS
Delivery rider

    What are the requirements of a CH•AN•JO rider?
  • A rider must be at least 18 years old, male or female, with a valid driver’s license. They must know their way around Metro Manila. Submit a clear scanned copy of the following to ask@pickaroo.com:
    • CV or Biodata
    • NBI or Police Clearance
    • Driver’s license
    • OR/CR of motorcycle owned
    • OR authorization from motorcycle owner with OR/CR
    • If the motorcycle is newly bought, a deed of sale or proof of purchase is needed.
    How do I apply as a rider?
  • Interested riders and shoppers can email their inquiries to ask@chanjo.com.
